About Us

Lycée Notre Dame de la Visitation de Rulindo is a prestigious educational institution in Rwanda. We aim to provide quality education and a nurturing environment for our students, ensuring their academic and personal development.

Our school is deeply committed to fostering values such as integrity, respect, and leadership. We are dedicated to preparing students for future success in both their careers and personal lives.

Academics

At Lycée Notre Dame de la Visitation de Rulindo, we offer a comprehensive academic curriculum that includes:

  • Primary Education
  • Secondary Education (O-Level and A-Level)
  • Language and Cultural Studies
  • STEM Programs
  • Arts and Humanities

Our dedicated teachers provide personalized support to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Admissions

Interested in joining Lycée Notre Dame de la Visitation de Rulindo? Our admissions process is simple and transparent. To apply, please follow these

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit necessary documents (e.g., academic transcripts, identification)
  3. Attend an interview (for secondary education applicants)
  4. Wait for the admission decision
